Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Finance 2025 ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your data.

1. Information We Collect

The App accesses your QuickBooks Online account to retrieve financial data such as invoices, transactions, and reports. No personal data unrelated to QuickBooks is collected.

2. How We Use the Data

The data is used only to display or export QuickBooks information into Microsoft Excel or other tools at your request. We do not share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

We do not permanently store your data on our servers. All data transfers are encrypted using secure APIs and industry-standard protocols.

4. Sharing of Data

We do not sell, rent, or disclose your information to any third party.

5. Security

We use OAuth 2.0 and HTTPS to protect your data during transmission. We do not store your QuickBooks credentials.

6. Your Consent

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
